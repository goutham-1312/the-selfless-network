Our Mission & Vision

Selfless Network – connecting hearts, hands, and hope.

Selfless Network is an open-source, non-profit community initiative born out of the necessity for unified, real-time civic mobilization in Tamil Nadu. Our platform bridges the gap between those who want to serve and those in urgent need of humanitarian support.

What We Believe In

  • Humanity First: Kindness transcends all boundaries. We support social actions that promote equality and empathy.
  • Environmental Awareness: A green planet is essential for healthy communities. We promote tree planting, plastic cleanups, and organic restoration.
  • Disaster Readiness: Empowering local citizens to react instantly during floods, fires, water scarcity, or health crises before institutional relief arrives.

Tamil Nadu Coverage

Our platform supports all 38 districts of Tamil Nadu, enabling granular location matchings based on Taluk and Village level directories. By routing alerts and notifications locally, we ensure help is fast, verified, and responsible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about Selfless Network.

How does volunteer matching work?
When a user submits a help request or a verified community alert is posted, volunteers registered in the exact same district are instantly notified inside the website. In the future, we will roll out SMS and WhatsApp integrations for real-time offline alerts.
Is my personal contact details visible to anyone?
No. To prevent spam and maintain privacy, your telephone number and email address are never displayed on the public Volunteer Directory. Registered users can only contact you via an anonymous click-to-contact routing or once you accept a help ticket.
What does "Pending Verification" mean on alerts?
To prevent fake news or duplicate reports, all community alerts submitted by the public are queued. Administrators verify them using local contact networks before marking them as "Verified" and broadcasting them to local volunteers.
How can I become an Administrator?
Admin roles are reserved for NGO coordinators, government emergency representatives, and vetted community leads. If you represent an organization, contact us directly via the contact page with your request.
